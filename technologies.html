Cutting Edge Surveying Technology

At Delta Geospatial, we pride ourselves on being at the forefront of surveying technologies and best practices.

Total Stations

A total station (TS) or total station theodolite (TST) is an electronic/optical instrument used for surveying and building construction. It is an electronic transit theodolite integrated with electronic distance measurement (EDM) to measure both vertical and horizontal angles and the slope distance from the instrument to a particular point.

Laser Scanners

3D scanning is the process of analyzing a real-world object or environment to collect three dimensional data of its shape and possibly its appearance (e.g. color). The collected data can then be used to construct digital 3D models.

AutoCad

AutoCAD is a commercial computer-aided design (CAD) and drafting software application. Developed and marketed by Autodesk,[1] AutoCAD was first released in December 1982 as a desktop app running on microcomputers with internal graphics controllers.

DJI Phantom Drones

The Phantom series has evolved to integrated flight programming with a camera, Wi-Fi, and the pilot's mobile device. Phantoms are made for aerial cinematography and photography applications, but are also used in recreational use.
